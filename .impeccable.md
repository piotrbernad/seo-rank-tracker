## Design Context

### Users
SEO professionals and agency owners tracking keyword positions for client domains. Used primarily at a desk during focused work sessions — checking ranks, adding keywords, reviewing SERP data. Data-dense work where speed and clarity matter.

### Brand Personality
Precise, technical, utilitarian. The tool should feel like a sharp instrument — no fluff, no decoration for decoration's sake. Confident and competent.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference**: agentmail.to — dark mode, monospace labels, terminal-inspired UI, corner bracket decorations, generous whitespace between sections with tight data density within tables
- **Theme**: Dark only
- **Tone**: Technical dashboard. Data-forward. Monospace for labels/metadata, proportional for content.
- **Anti-references**: Colorful marketing dashboards, gradient-heavy SaaS UIs, rounded bubbly card grids

### Design Principles
1. **Data first** — the interface exists to show positions and URLs, not to look pretty
2. **Terminal-inspired** — monospace labels, uppercase section headers, subtle dotted borders, corner bracket accents
3. **Generous breathing room** — spacious between sections, tight within data rows
4. **Muted palette** — near-black backgrounds, white text, minimal accent color (only for position badges and actions)
5. **No decoration without purpose** — every visual element communicates something
